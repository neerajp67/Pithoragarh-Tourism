Winter In Pithoragarh

During winters, the days are quite chilly and have an average minimum temperature that generally touches almost 4°C.

Spring In Pithoragarh

April to September, the climate is comfortable and ideal for visiting temples.

Summer In Pithoragarh

Generally in the summers, the city witnesses temperature ranging from 7-20°C. Summers are considered as best time to visit Pithoragarh and to see all attractive spots including temples.

Monsoon In Pithoragarh

The days of monsoon seasons are quite pleasant and generally nights are chilly.

Autumn In Pithoragarh

September to November the scenery changes drastically and most eye-catching views appear

Autumn-winter In Pithoragarh

From December to March, it is freezing cold and heavy snowfall can be seen. Those who relish this and arrive in heavy woolens can surely enjoy.

Welcome to Mini kashmir Pithoragarh

"Pithoragarh district is the easternmost Himalayan district in the state of Uttarakhand, India. It is naturally landscaped with high Himalayan mountains, snowcapped peaks, passes, valleys, alpine meadows, forests, waterfalls, perennial rivers, glaciers, and springs. The flora and fauna of the area have rich ecological diversity. Pithoragarh has many temples and ruined forts from the once flourishing reign of the warrior Chand Kingdom. "The district is named after its headquarters town, Pithoragarh. Tradition has it that during the reign of the Chand Rajas of Kumaon, one Piru, also called Prithvi Gosain, built a fort here and named it Prithvigarh which, in the course of time, got changed into Pithoragarh"

The Pithoragarh town is located at a height of 1645 meters above sea level. The district lies between 29.4° to 30.3° North latitude and 80° to 81° East longitude along the eastern and southern part of the central Himalayas with Indo-Tibbetan watershed divide in the north and the Kali river forming a continuous border with Nepal in the east. The Pithoragarh district is surrounded by the national boundaries of Almora, Champawat, Bageshwar and Chamoli districts and extends over an area of 7,217.7 sq. Kms.
